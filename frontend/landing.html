How it works

ErgoPilot uses computer vision to estimate worker posture and translate it into familiar ergonomics scores.

  1. Set up a camera. Point a standard webcam at the workstation—no depth sensors or wearables.
  2. Calibrate neutral posture. Capture a baseline stance for each worker to personalize feedback.
  3. Record tasks. Start a session and let ErgoPilot flag lifts that exceed safe thresholds.
  4. Review clips. Safety leads and workers review high‑risk clips together and agree on specific improvements.
